Hi, I’m
Sujan Doshi.

Building enduring consumer brands with a focus on product, operations, and customer trust. Currently at LuvMyJewelry.

Current Focus

LuvMyJewelry (LMJ)

A modern jewelry brand built to become a trusted, everyday destination for accessible, high-quality jewelry. Today, LMJ partners with select retailers while continuing to grow its direct-to-consumer presence.

Our focus is on building a brand that operates consistently at scale and delivers a strong, dependable customer experience.

Philosophy

"I care deeply about clarity, consistency, and making decisions that compound over the long term."

I spend most of my time thinking about how enduring consumer brands are built, from product and operations to distribution and customer trust.

Background

Earlier in my career, I worked in consulting at Deloitte Consulting and in finance at D.E. Shaw & Co.

Those experiences continue to shape how I approach problem-solving, decision-making, and disciplined execution.

Personal

Outside of work, you can usually find me running in Central Park, reading a good mystery or suspense novel, or unwinding over a cup of coffee at the latest coffee shop in town.

Most importantly, I love spending time with my wife, daughter, and family. They are a constant reminder of what matters most.
